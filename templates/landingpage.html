Patient History

Medical records management platform



Features

New patient registration form

Register new patients by providing the required information. Note that card number is automatically generated and it can be accessed by the "Get Info" search button by providing the patient's phone number.

Appointment session form

Each appointment session is recorded here. Doctors will populate the form with the necessary information about the patient.

Display patient's records

Doctors can view the patient's records by using the search form which takes card number as input.

This is a portfolio project which is part of our software engineering training at ALX Africa. This project was inspired by incidents that occured in a year long war which took place in the northern part of Ethiopia. This war was very destructive to the extent of vandalizing hospitals and clinincs. Media were reporting that many years worth of medical records were turned into ashes leaving patients with no medical hostory at all. This sad reality inspired us to start this project and hopefully contribute to the digitization of the health sector.
